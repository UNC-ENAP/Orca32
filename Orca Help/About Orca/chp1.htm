About Orca

Orca evolved from SHaRC, an Object-Oriented DAQ programming project carried out by John Wilkerson and Frank McGirt at LANL in the 1980's and futher refined by Mark Howe and others at the Center for Nuclear Physics and Astrophysics (CENPA) at the University of Washington during the 1990's.

Orca is written in Obj-C using the Apple developer tools. Work on Orca began in ernest in Nov 2002 at CENPA under the direction of John Wilkerson.
